About Me

I am currently a enrolled at Pennsylvania College of Technology. I am a senior in the Web & Interactive Media major. I chose to engage in this field of study because once my high school web design teacher let us mess around with the basics of CSS, I never stopped wanting to improve my skills in everything website design. Since then I knew that I was going to be interested in the web, wanting to take on the challenges it has to offer, and putting websites out on the Internet.

On my down time, I like to enjoy time at the gym to just think about anything that is on my mind. If I am not at the gym, then I am probably playing video games to fill time up. I am a gamer, but not as much as I used to be.

I am always learning. From the basics of making food for myself to things that are meaningful to what I believe life is all about. Sometimes technology is my best friend, but then I go out and socialize with the real world and it gets neglected and ignored. I love hanging out with friends and being outdoors. I do my best to keep a positive mindset no matter how much stress I am under. We all have stress, but nobody has time for negativity.
